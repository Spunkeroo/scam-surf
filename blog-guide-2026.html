How to Spot Surf Industry Scams & Stay Safe Online in 2026

By scam.surf | February 21, 2026 | 8 min read

From counterfeit surfboards to fake surf schools and dangerous knock-off gear, surf industry scams put both your wallet and safety at risk. Here's how to protect yourself.

Common Surf Scams

  • Counterfeit boards — fake brand surfboards with weak construction
  • Fake surf schools — unlicensed, uninsured operations
  • Knockoff wax and leashes — cheap leashes that snap in overhead surf
  • Vacation rental fraud — fake beachfront property listings
  • Online browsing scams — phishing sites disguised as surf shops

Protection Tips

  • Buy boards from authorized dealers or directly from shapers
  • Verify surf school licensing and insurance
  • Never buy safety equipment from unverified sellers
  • Use a VPN router for safe browsing
