Counterfeit Surfboard Brands

Counterfeit

Fake Firewire, Channel Islands, and other premium boards sold online at 50-70% off. Made with cheap foam and fiberglass that delaminates quickly. Some snap in normal waves.

High Risk Active

Fake Surf Camp Booking Sites

Booking Scam

Professional-looking websites advertising surf camps in Bali, Costa Rica, and Portugal. Collect full payment ($1,500-5,000) then don't exist. Stolen photos from real camps.

High Risk Active

Unlicensed Surf Schools

Instructor Fraud

Surf schools with no ISA certification, no insurance, no lifeguard training. Instructors can't perform rescues. Multiple drowning incidents linked to unlicensed operations.

Medium Risk Active

Fake Pro Surfer Gear Endorsements

Influencer Scam

Brands using AI-generated or unauthorized pro surfer endorsements to sell gear. Surfers never tested or approved the products.

Medium Risk Active

Wetsuit Sizing Scam Returns

Return Fraud

Online wetsuit sellers with intentionally wrong sizing charts. When the suit doesn't fit, return shipping costs more than the suit. No refund on 'worn' items.

Low Risk Active

Surf Forecast Premium Scams

Subscription Scam

Apps charging $20-40/month for surf forecasts available free on Surfline, MSW, and Windy. Repackage free data with a fancy UI. Auto-renew with difficult cancellation.

High Risk Active

Stolen Board Resale Rings

Theft

Organized theft of surfboards from beach racks and cars, resold on Craigslist and Facebook Marketplace. Often repainted or re-labeled to avoid identification.

Medium Risk Active

Fake Surf Photography Prints

Copyright Theft

Stolen surf photography sold as prints on Etsy and Instagram. Original photographers receive no credit or payment. Prints are low-quality reproductions.

High Risk Active

Wave Pool Investment Schemes

Investment Fraud

Fake wave pool development projects seeking investors ($10k-100k minimum). Promise 'guaranteed returns' from the surf tourism boom. Projects never break ground.
